A file morse_one.txt was given.
The contents of morse_one.txt was a sequence of D, B, and S.

I obtained a message by substituting BSD to - . and applying "From Morse Code" on CyberChef.

Substitute, From Morse Code - CyberChef

指示に従ってメッセージにflag{}を加えることで、flagが得られた。
